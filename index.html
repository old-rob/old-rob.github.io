I'm a software engineer with a background in bioinformatics and a strong focus on full-stack development, data engineering, and scientific computing. I have experience with cloud deployments on AWS, containerization, REST API development, and maintaining large-scale data workflows. I’m fluent in Spanish and conversational in German, and I bring strong communication and documentation skills from working across multidisciplinary teams. My technical foundation includes C++, Java, and R, with additional strengths in version control, data parsing, and reproducible research environments.

# Creator of nphd.top

I designed, built, and deployed **[nphd.top](https://nphd.top)** — a minimal, fast, and focused website that highlights the value of pursuing intellectual work without requiring a PhD. The site serves as a manifesto, a resource, and a provocation, offering a clear alternative path for thinkers, builders, and researchers outside academia.

## Key Contributions

### Design and UX

- Developed a **distraction-free, typographically focused interface** that emphasizes clarity, speed, and message delivery.
- Built a custom layout from scratch to reflect the philosophy of the site: minimal structure, maximal signal.
- **Designed the site's custom typeface**, reinforcing the site's independent, hand-crafted aesthetic and voice.

### Development and Deployment

- Hand-coded the entire site with **clean, semantic HTML and CSS**, optimizing for performance, readability, and accessibility.
- Deployed the site with a **zero-dependency stack**, ensuring stability and maintainability with no external frameworks or libraries.
- Set up custom domain routing and HTTPS to deliver a fast, secure browsing experience.

### Content and Messaging

- Authored all content on the site, distilling a complex critique of credentialism into a **succinct and shareable format**.
- Structured the site to invite exploration, conversation, and reflection — without relying on blogs, social feeds, or comment sections.

## Impact

**nphd.top** has reached a growing audience of independent researchers, hackers, and autodidacts. It stands as an example of how web development, typography, and thoughtful content can combine to express bold ideas without technical bloat or institutional affiliation.

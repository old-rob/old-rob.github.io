# Creator of nphd.top

I designed, built, and deployed **[nphd.top](https://nphd.top)** — a minimal, fast, and fun website that displays comics and includes an ecommerce store.

## Key Contributions

### Design and UX

- Built a custom layout from scratch to reflect the philosophy of the site: minimal structure, maximal signal.
- **Designed the site's custom typeface**, reinforcing the site's independent, hand-crafted aesthetic and voice.

### Development and Deployment

- Coded the entire site with **clean, semantic HTML and CSS**, optimizing for performance, readability, and accessibility.
- Deployed the site with a **zero-dependency stack**, ensuring stability and maintainability with no external frameworks or libraries.
- Set up custom domain routing and HTTPS to deliver a fast, secure browsing experience.

### Content and Messaging

- Authored all content on the site, distilling a complex critique of credentialism into a **succinct and shareable format**.
- Structured the site to invite exploration, conversation, and reflection — without relying on blogs, social feeds, or comment sections.
